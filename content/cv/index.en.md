*critically shaping the digital (r)evolution*

## Research Interests

Digital Humanities, application and theory of machine learning in the humanities, digital history, epistemology and theory of Digital Humanities, editing and archiving techniques, media and media theory (pre-modern and post-modern), history of record-keeping, digital teaching methods, pre-modern administration, auxiliary sciences of history.

## Education

**2024** — Habilitation (cumulative) in Digital Humanities and Auxiliary Sciences of History: "Texterkennung und Textanalyse: Methodische Evolution durch maschinelle Lernverfahren", University of Bern. Defended 8 November 2024.

**2016** — PhD in History: "Schriftordnungen im Wandel. Produktions-, Gebrauchs- und Aufbewahrungspraktiken von klösterlichem Schriftgut in Königsfelden (1300–1600)", [University of Zurich](https://www.uzh.ch/). Supervised by Prof. Dr. Simon Teuscher (UZH) & Prof. Dr. Adam Kosto ([Columbia University](https://www.columbia.edu/)). Defended 11 November 2016 (*summa cum laude*). Published 2020.

**2010** — Licentiatus Philosophiae (lic. phil.) in General History and German Literature, [University of Zurich](https://www.uzh.ch/).
